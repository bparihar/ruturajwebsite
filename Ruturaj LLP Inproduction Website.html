Who We Are

India's Trusted Agricultural Export Partner

Ruturaj Enterprise LLP is a Mumbai-based agricultural trading and export company, established in 2008. We specialize in sourcing, processing, and exporting premium-grade agricultural commodities from India's most productive farming regions to buyers across the Middle East, Africa, Europe, and Southeast Asia.

"From the cashew orchards of Goa to the basmati fields of Punjab — we bring India's agricultural richness to your doorstep."

Our team of 80+ agri-trade professionals manages procurement, quality control, documentation, and logistics — ensuring every shipment meets international standards and buyer expectations.

Our Principles

What We Stand For

Every business decision at Ruturaj Enterprise is guided by these four commitments to our farmers, buyers, and the environment.

🌱 Ethical Farm Sourcing

We pay fair prices to farmers, ensure sustainable harvest practices, and maintain complete supply chain traceability from farm to freight.

🔬 Zero-Compromise Quality

NABL-accredited lab testing, moisture checks, aflatoxin screening, and physical grading at every stage — before we sign any shipment off.

📜 Full Compliance

From APEDA registration to phytosanitary certificates, we handle all Indian export regulations and buyer-country import requirements in-house.

🤝 Buyer-First Service

Dedicated account managers, real-time shipment tracking, 24-hour response SLA, and proactive communication on every order.
